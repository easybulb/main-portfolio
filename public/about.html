My journey into the world of technology began at the age of 16 in Nigeria, where I started my career as a computer hardware technician in local cyber cafes. This early exposure not only sparked my passion for computers but also laid the foundational skills that I have continually built upon. Since then, I have set my sights on becoming an accomplished Full Stack Developer, with the capability to build and manage both the front-end and back-end of web applications.

From my beginnings in hardware, I quickly became fascinated with the creative possibilities of software. The aspiration to master both ends of development stems from a profound passion for technology and innovation. Over the years, I have designed and hosted numerous websites using platforms like WIX, Hostinger, and GoDaddy, each time refining my skills in web design and functionality.

A pivotal moment in my journey was the creation of Easybulb, a project that developed smart lighting solutions controllable via mobile devices. The project allowed me to immerse myself in the entire product development cycle, from conceptualization to market launch. It required me to solve complex technical challenges, ensuring that the lighting system was not only functional but also user-friendly. This innovative solution garnered significant recognition, ultimately propelling me onto the BBC2 program "Dragons Den," (link to Dragons Den) where I had the privilege to showcase my product. The success of Easybulb stands as a testament to my capability to lead significant tech projects from concept to market. My entrepreneurial spirit drove me to tackle the challenges of merging software and hardware into one seamless user experience.

What distinguishes me from other candidates is my blend of technical skills and leadership experience. Having directed three companies, I bring managerial acumen alongside my technical expertise. My successful integration of various e-commerce and payment gateway solutions into these platforms further demonstrates my technical proficiency and adaptability. Whether designing a robust payment system or a mobile-friendly website, I have always aimed for high-quality solutions that prioritize user experience.
